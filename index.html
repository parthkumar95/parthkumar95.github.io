Parth Kumar's Research Website

I am a Physics PhD candidate at the University of Arizona working on Nonequilibrium Quantum Dynamics, expecting to graduate in Spring 2024. I am actively seeking postdoctoral positions broadly in the field of Open Quantum Systems theory.

The core of my doctoral work addresses the question of how to partition the energy and entropy of time-dependent open quantum systems in a thermodynamically consistent manner—a problem which has attracted much debate and discussion in recent years. Additionally, I have worked on quantum information engines, topological effects and fluctuation theorems in open quantum systems during the course of my research.

I am advised by Prof. Charles A. Stafford.
